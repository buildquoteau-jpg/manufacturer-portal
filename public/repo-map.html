buildquote.com.au  ·  RFQ platform for builders
Purpose: The public-facing RFQ platform. Builders arrive here, build or upload a materials list, select a supplier, and submit an RFQ. It reads from the shared Supabase database and receives draft items created in the manufacturer portal.

manufacturer-portal
mfp.buildquote.com.au  ·  Manufacturer & supplier portal
Purpose: The back-office and public product browser. Manufacturers manage their product data, suppliers manage their widget configs, and builders browse products and select items to add to a draft RFQ — which then passes to buildquote.com.au via a shared draft ID in the URL.
